Reason for Site Name

The name "Sole Haven" evokes a sense of comfort and refuge for shoe enthusiasts. It suggests a place where visitors can find the perfect pair of shoes that suits their style and needs

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide an online platform for customers to browse, select, and purchase a wide variety of shoes, ranging from casual to formal, athletic to trendy. It aims to offer an engaging shopping experience with detailed product descriptions, reviews, and easy navigation.

Visitor Scenarios

  • Casual Browsing: A visitor casually explores the latest shoe trends and collections without a specific purchase in mind. Searching for a Specific Pair: A user looks for a specific brand or style of shoes for an upcoming event. Comparing Options: A customer compares different shoes based on features, prices, and reviews to make an informed decision. Making a Purchase: A visitor is ready to buy and goes through the checkout process, requiring a smooth and secure transaction experience. Seeking Recommendations: A user looks for style recommendations based on their preferences or occasions.
  • A visitor is searching for children's birthday offers and discounts for a shoes arrangement gift.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #E91E63 (Used for buttons and headings to create a vibrant look)

Secondary Color: #4CAF50 (Used for accents such as footer and product labels)

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display, serif (used for headings to give a formal and elegant feel)

Body Font: Lato, sans-serif (used for body text for better readability)
